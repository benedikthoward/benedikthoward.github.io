Role: Test & Data When: 2022–2023 Focus: Battery testing & characterization Stack: Python, C, CAN, DAQ

Overview

Work centered on battery testing and characterization—acquiring clean data, extracting performance metrics, and informing safety limits for the racecar’s energy system.

Highlights

  • Built repeatable test procedures to capture pack and module behavior under varied load profiles.
  • Logged and decoded CAN streams; synchronized with DAQ measurements for aligned analysis.
  • Produced summary metrics (IR, thermal response) for quick comparisons.
  • Outlined safe envelopes and edge cases to feed into integration and race-day ops.
  • Complete comprehensive high voltage saftey training
